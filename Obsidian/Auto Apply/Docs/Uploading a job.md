Auto Apply uses a browser extension to scrape job data from LinkedIn.
Navigate to LinkedIn's jobs listing once the browser extension is installed. 
Click the "Scrape" button injected onto the job cards in the left column of LinkedIn.
The job should appear on localhost:8000 momentarily
## Installing the Extension

### Firefox Install Instructions
1. In your browser's url bar, type: ```about:debugging```
2. Select "This Firefox" from the tabs on the left sidebar
3. Click "Load Temporary Add-on..."
4. Select auto_apply/browser-extension/manifest.json
